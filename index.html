About ECAN

The leading umbrella organization of Nepal's consultancy sector

Established in 1997 and guided by integrity, professionalism, and responsible counseling, ECAN promotes ethical student mobility, strengthens member consultancies, and connects Nepali students with trusted global education opportunities.

  • Ethical student mobilityResponsible, transparent counselling across the sector.
  • Stronger membersProfessional development and a binding code of conduct.
  • Policy advocacyDialogue with government and key stakeholders.
  • Global connectionLinking students to quality providers worldwide.
Mission & Standards

Built on trust since 1997

ECAN strengthens Nepal's educational consultancy sector through ethical practice, professional development, policy dialogue, and responsible global student mobility.

Responsible counselling

Promoting transparent practices and professional standards across every member consultancy.

Trusted connections

Building bridges between Nepali consultancies, international institutions, service providers, and students.

Sustainable mobility

Ensuring student mobility that is ethical, sustainable, and centered on the student's best interest.

Our Mandate

Controlling, monitoring and nurturing Nepal's environment for international education

Registered in 1997, ECAN exists to bring proper information and guidance to parents and students considering studies abroad — an active association of professionals working to keep the sector safe and credible.

Why It Matters

Behind every journey lies a dream, a family's trust, and a nation's future.

Every year, thousands of young Nepalis leave home in pursuit of global opportunities. We are committed to ensuring that this journey is guided by transparency, accountability, and student-centered protection.

Our Vision

Opportunity and security, hand in hand.

To create an international education ecosystem where opportunity and security go hand in hand — empowering our youth to achieve their ambitions with confidence and peace of mind.
